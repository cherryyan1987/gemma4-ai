# Gemma 4 AI

Gemma 4 AI is an open-source AI playground focused on developers, creators, and AI enthusiasts.

Gemma 4 AI helps users explore powerful AI capabilities in one place, including:

- Coding and reasoning workflows
- Multimodal AI experiences
- Upcoming AI image generation
- Upcoming AI video generation
- Free and paid plans
